About us

English Playgroup Innsbruck

The group meets every Wednesday from 3pm to 4.30pm at the Stadtteiltreff Höttinger Au. The aims of the group are simple:

  • to offer a wide range of toys, puzzles, books, etc to kids aged 0-6, and give them the opportunity to interact and form new friendships in English.

  • To offer a small library of English kids books for families to borrow books free of charge

  • to be supportive to Mummies, Daddies and Grandparents. The opportunity to speak to another adult(yay!) - and a chance to have a cup of coffee(donation based).

  • To offer occasional weekend events like the Family Cooking Club or seasonal parties so the whole family can attend

Note: While this group was originally created for families where English is one of the home languages, you don’t need to be English speakers to attend. Everyone is welcome. For more information, email englishplaygroupinnsbruck@gmail.com

Family Cooking Club

Dates

The idea is simple: The Cooking Club is an event where families come together for lunch. There is a theme that is announced in advance, e.g. Tyrolean, comfort food etc., and then each family brings a dish based on this theme. Everyone eats together and afterwards the children can play and the parents can sit together over a coffee and have a chat.

Please share what you are planning to make a couple of days in advance and what category it is(dessert, side, starter, main etc), so we get a general idea of what everyone is bringing. Don't worry, you can always change your mind if you have a brilliant idea last minute.

There will be a stove and microwave available in case you need to heat anything up. If you have any allergies, please make them known so people can be aware. Looking forward to a yummy meal and great get together!
